About Us

We are Prisoner Skills, an organization that focuses on assisting incarcerated individuals with rehabilitation through job employment during their sentences. Many times prisons are heavily populated with people, and oftentimes these people have valuable skills that can be of use to one's community. Inmates with low threat levels/good behaviour can be given the option to participate in this program. Employers with a job they need done can then browse through candidates with relative skills to the employers job and hire that individual. Just because someone has ended up in prison does not necessarily render them useless or unimportant to the community. There are many tasks out there that these prisoners more than capable of doing right.

We feel that by giving prisoners a job, we are also giving them a purpose in today's society no matter how big or small it is. Often times prisons do not have much for prisoners to do/participate in. We feel that by giving prisoners a task to accomplish that will end up benefiting their community will give them a sense of pride and accomplishment. By participating in our program prisoners are also more likely to find a job after serving their sentences. Many times people get out of prison and can not find a legitimate job to support themselves or their family and end up going down the same path that got them in prison in the first place. By easing them into society and allowing them to gain some work experience, we aim to stop this self destructive cycle.

How it works?

Our goal is to find you the right person for the right job. We match employers with jobs they need done with prisoners who have the skills to get those jobs done. Employers can view a prisons database of eligible candidates for hire and their skills.
