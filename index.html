Find the right person, for the right job!

We are Prisoner Skills, an organization that focuses on assisting incarcerated individuals with rehabilitation through job employment during their sentences. Many times prisons are heavily populated with people, and oftentimes these people have valuable skills that can be of use to one's community. Inmates with low threat levels/good behaviour can be given the option to participate in this program. Employers with a job they need done can then browse through candidates with relative skills to the employers job and hire that individual. Just because someone has ended up in prison does not necessarily render them useless or unimportant to the community. There are many tasks out there that these prisoners more than capable of doing right.
